
                        EXECUTIVE SUMMARY



                        This report analyzes customer spending, product category performance,
                        employee sales performance, and revenue distribution by country
                        using the Northwind database.


                        The objective is to identify key revenue drivers and provide
                        business recommendations based on the available data.



                        CUSTOMER ANALYSIS



                        The top 3 customers accounted for 41.91% of the total revenue.

                        indicating that a significant portion of sales comes from a small
                        group of strategic customers.


                        The highest-value customer was Ernst Handel, generating $38031.21 and representing
                        18.27% of the total revenue analyzed.


                        Additionally, The average customer spending is $20817.521



                        CATEGORY ANALYSIS



                        The category with the highest revenue was Beverages.


                        This category generated $115832.5 and represented 28.76% of the total category revenue.


                        This suggests that the category plays a major role in the company's
                        overall sales performance.



                        EMPLOYEE ANALYSIS



                        The highest-performing employee was Margaret Peacock.


                        This employee generated $111888.5 and contributed 27.78% of the total employee revenue analyzed.


                        The top five employees represented 73.47% of total revenue,
                        showing that sales performance is concentrated among a limited
                        group of employees.



                        GEOGRAPHIC ANALYSIS



                        The country with the highest revenue was USA.


                        This market generated $72971.75 and accounted for 21.11% of the revenue analyzed.


                        The results suggest that this country represents one of the company's
                        most important markets.



                        RECOMMENDATIONS



                        Maintain strong relationships with high-value customers.



                        Increase investment in top-performing categories.



                        Study the sales strategies of top-performing employees.



                        Strengthen marketing efforts in high-revenue countries.



                        Monitor revenue concentration to reduce dependence on a small number of customers.




                        FINAL CONCLUSION



                        The analysis identified the main customers, categories, employees,
                        and countries responsible for generating revenue.

                        These findings can help the company focus resources on its strongest
                        areas while improving opportunities in lower-performing segments.
